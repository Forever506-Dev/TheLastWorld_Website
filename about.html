About The Outbreak

Your city fell in 24 hours. Power is gone, streets are infested and only a few organized survivors remain. This server drops you in the middle of that chaos with hardcore survival mechanics.

Server Features

  • QBCore-based progression with scarcity-focused economy and permadeath options.
  • Dynamic horde events, supply drops, and safe zones controlled by players.
  • Custom loot, crafting and infection systems tailored for long-term survival.

Who We Are

We are a small group of developers and role‑players from Québec obsessed with building an immersive zombie survival FiveM experience where every decision matters.
